DIY Newbie Tips
Essential safety & setup advice
▼
🔴
Fuse within 18 inches of battery — install on every positive wire leaving the battery. Prevents short-circuit fires.
🔌
Use proper lugs — crimp copper ring terminals. Loose connections cause heat, voltage drop, and fire.
⚡
Wire battery LAST — connect all components first, then the battery, to prevent accidental shorts.
🌡️
Batteries hate heat — keep in a cool, ventilated area. LiFePO4 is safer but still prefers cool temps.
☀️
Tilt panels 15–20° facing true south for best year-round output in PH (latitude 5°–20°N).
📊
Oversize panels by 20–30% — real conditions (heat, dust, clouds) reduce output significantly.
🔗
Series = higher V, same A. Parallel = same V, higher A. Higher voltage strings need thinner wire.
🔋
Never mix old and new batteries in a bank. Use matched brand, age, and capacity always.
📱
Get a battery monitor — don't guess state of charge. A BMS display prevents deep discharge damage.
🌧️
Plan for 3 cloudy days in rainy season (July–Sept). Don't size too lean for typhoon season.
